Here at MID (My Interior Designer) we're not merely interior designers . We are storytellers with a paintbrush, dream weavers, and emotion architects. In our opinion, your house ought to be a vivid representation of who you area place where happiness blossoms and memories are forged.

Our method is a cooperative dance. We pay close attention to your goals, desires, and even the way the afternoon sun streams through your windows. We create a symphony of textures, materials, and furniture that sings in perfect tune with your spirit through brainstorming and sketching.However, it goes beyond appearances. We are aware of the beating heart inside your walls. We design environments that encourage connections, fun, and serve as the stage for some of life's most treasured memories.

MID was created in 2010 and is a vivid thread in the colorful tapestry of American design. It was a gentle seed planted in the heart rather than a dramatic entrance. Inspired by a common love of places that tell tales and enrich lives, our founders set out to change experiences as much as interiors.
